    About Us

    Snaga Pack’s mission is to take on food insecurity, by making food banks more accessible. Our innovative “ticket system” approach makes it possible for food banks and community members to organize efficient food pick ups, taking another step to a hunger-free future.

    Our Values

    Availability

    We strive to connect people with food banks that have their resources in stock. We prioritize availability for everyone; no one should have to go hungry.

    Access

    Making food banks known and accessible is what we do best. Finding food near you has never been easier!

    Quality

    The quality of your food is just as important as quanity! Connecting yourself to a wide range of food banks is a great way to adopt a diverse, healthy diet!

    10% of the world goes hungry every day. Let’s make a difference.
